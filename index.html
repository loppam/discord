What is UberEats?

UberEats is a food delivery platform that allows you to order food from your favorite restaurants and have it delivered to your door. However, using UberEats can be expensive due to their delivery fees and markup on restaurant prices.

What is Postmates?

Postmates is a delivery platform that allows you to order food, groceries, and other items from your favorite local stores and have them delivered to your door. However, using Postmates can also be expensive due to their delivery fees and markup on store prices.
